2019 ANCHORAGE PROPOSITION RESULT MAPS

 

    Longer descriptions of the ballots can be found here, on the municipal website.

    I've put links at each proposition with a map to the precinct election results.  The palate colors of the precincts are broken into five colors.  Red representing the 20% of precincts voting with the least percentage of YES votes and Blue being the 20% of precincts with the most YES votes.  Red-purple, Purple, and Blue-purple are the precincts with the middle 60% of precincts. Keep in mind that purple does not necessarily mean the precinct voted near even YES and NO
